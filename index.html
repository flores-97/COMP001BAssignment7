Assignment 7

Feedback

What worked well over the last eight weeks and aided your learning process?

What aided me during theses eight weeks were the examples and visual learning aids. Being able to see what was being asked for the week's assignment was a great help.

What would you change about the course?

If I were to add or change anything about the class it would be adding a follow along video showing how you create and complete the assignment like you do during the class. I think it would be very helpful as some of the slides are a little hard to understand.

Did you learn what you expected to learn in this course?

I do believe I learned what was described during the syllabus. I also think I learned more than I expected too. After this course I can visualize what I want to create better and allows me to create what I want with better understanding.

What concepts were new to you, and what concepts have you already encountered in the past?

Learning about the grid was new to me. Until this course I could not comprehend how sections were separated. Using a grid seems to be the easiest way to create a section on a page in my opinion.
